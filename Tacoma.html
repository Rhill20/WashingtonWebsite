Welcome to Tacoma

Tacoma is located in Western Washington on the Puget Sound. Tacoma is a large city with a variety of different cultures. Tacoma is well-known for its port, this port is one of the largest in North America

Facts about Tacoma

  • Tacoma is located in Western Washington
  • The population is just over 219,000
  • Tacoma was established in 1875
  • Tacoma is a Urban Environment
  • The average Household income is $77,141, this is under the state average of $81,245

Fun things to do in Tacoma

  • Visit the Museum of Glass
  • Visit Point Defiance Zoo and Aquarium
  • Visit the Tacoma Art Museum
  • Go to a Event in the Tacoma Dome
  • Experience the Nightlife
