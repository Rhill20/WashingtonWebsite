Welcome to Raymond

Raymond Washington is located in the South West of Washington. This is a small town that historically was tied to the timber industry.

Facts about Raymond

  • Raymond is located in the South West of Washington
  • The population is 3 thousand
  • Raymond was established in 1907
  • Raymond is a rural environment
  • The average household income is $55,593, this is almost $20,000 under the state average of $81,245

Fun things to do in Raymond

  • Fish on the Willapa River
  • Hike on the Willapa Trail
  • Explore Downtown
  • Go Bird Watching
  • Check the Community Board and Attend Local Events
